Book Details

Genre: Autobiography

About the Book

In the high-stakes world of Washington, D.C.’s elite cigar lounges, smoke is more than atmosphere. It is cover. Julian Khan has spent years building The Oasis, a sanctuary of wealth, influence, and quiet control. But when charismatic rival Marcus Delacroix opens The Black Diamond, a glamorous new lounge that threatens Julian’s reign, a fierce turf war ignites between two men determined to rule the city’s most dangerous luxury scene.

As competition turns ruthless, the battle spreads far beyond the lounge floor. Beneath the polished image of premium cigars, expensive liquor, and political connections lies a volatile underworld of betrayal, corruption, seduction, and revenge. And when family loyalty collides with ambition, no one escapes untouched.

With unforgettable characters, razor-sharp tension, and a world as seductive as it is deadly, Cigar Wars is a bold, cinematic crime drama where power burns slow… and revenge burns hotter.
